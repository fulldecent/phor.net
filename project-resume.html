Non-Fungible Tokens 2018–Current

The flagship use case for blockchain applications was money. First, Bitcoin created $300 billion dollars out of thin air. For the next ten years, the only actual useful applications of blockchain have been to purchase drugs, and to raise money quasi-legally. Then I got involved with the second use case.

Non-fungible tokens are a technology to track physical assets and in-game assets similar to the way that Bitcoin tracks money. The difference is that Bitcoin has zero intrinsic value and physical assets have much intrinsic value. My role has been to publish the standard, fight through the publication and acceptance process and advocate for this use case.

Here is what a pull request review from me looks like: Erasure Bay. I have also reviewed/worked on: 0xcert Framework, Nightfall, Solidity, MLB Champions. I have responsibly published vulnerabilities against Compound Finance (token prices can change faster than specified) and Aion (can steal anyone's tokens). I review interoperability standards, ERC-165, ERC-1167.
