Short (44 words)

Detailed (50 words)

William brings NFT applications to the world including government, enterprise and individual artists. He leads workshops to teach web3 concepts and teaches entry-level smart contract programming as well as advisory for the most technical projects. He is the lead author of ERC-721, the technology on which modern NFTs are launched.
