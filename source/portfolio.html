The seven-minute test

The critical step for attracting and exciting developers in your community is helping them compile the code. Here's the simple test: if an end user is slightly interested (but not religious) about your project, could they contribute something useful within seven minutes?

If so, great! Your community is growing. If not, you have a problem no amount of marketing can fix.

The seven-minute test is easy to say and hard to pass. It requires clear documentation, a working build system, a welcoming contribution process, and a product people actually want to touch.
